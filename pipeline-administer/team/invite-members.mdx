You can send email invitations to add new members to a project in Chronosphere
Telemetry Pipeline.

1. Sign in to [Telemetry Pipeline](https://core.calyptia.com/).
1. Open the project that you want to add members to.
1. Go to **<Icon icon="users" /> Team**, then click **Invite Members**.
1. In the **Email List** field, enter the email addresses of the members you want
   to invite. To invite multiple members, use commas to separate each email address.
1. Click **Invite**.

<Info>
All new members are assigned the viewer [role](/pipeline-administer/team#roles).
by default. After a member accepts your invitation, you can configure their settings
to assign them a different role.
</Info>
