Some Datadog widgets have corollaries in Chronosphere which can be recreated in
[Dashboards](/observe/dashboards) using panels. Use the following sections
to help recreate some previously used visuals.

## Stacked bar graph

To create a stacked bar graph similar to the Datadog widget:

1. In the Chronosphere web interface, create a
   [dashboard panel](/observe/dashboards/panels#add-panels-to-a-dashboard).
1. Under **Visualization**, click **Graph**.
1. Scroll to the **Display** section.
1. Use the toggle to disable **Lines**.
1. Use the toggle to enable **Stacks**.
1. Under **Hover tooltip**, set the **Sort order** to `decreasing`.
1. Click **Apply** to review your changes, or click **Save** to finish.

## Top list

To create a top list:

1. In the Chronosphere web interface, create a
   [dashboard panel](/observe/dashboards/panels#add-panels-to-a-dashboard).
1. Under **Visualization**, click **Table**.
1. At the bottom of the page, click the **Transform** tab.
1. Click **Reduce**.
1. Change these values:
    - **Mode:** `Series to rows`
    - **Calculations:** `Total`
1. Click **Add transformation** and then add the following values:
    - Sort by
    - **Field:** `Total`
    - **Reverse:** `Enable`
1. Click **Apply** to review your changes, or click **Save** to finish.
